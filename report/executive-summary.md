# Executive Summary

Azure Crest Hospital, a major healthcare provider, experienced a
ransomware incident following the deployment of a cost-saving
in-house ERP system.

The attack originated from a phishing campaign delivering
malicious macro-enabled documents. Multiple employees interacted
with the files, resulting in malware execution, data discovery,
exfiltration, and ransomware encryption.

This investigation documents how the threat actor gained access,
expanded impact, and disrupted hospital operations.
